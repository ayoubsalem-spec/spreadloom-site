Inventory & supply

From one fragile file to a live, shared system

Before: One spreadsheet, manually updated, frequently out of sync between the warehouse floor and the office — leading to stockouts on fast-moving items and no early warning before it happened.

After: A live dashboard showing stock on hand, reorder points, and supplier info per item — with automatic low-stock flags instead of someone noticing too late.

Result: The team catches reorder points before they become stockouts, and everyone's looking at the same live numbers instead of whoever last emailed the spreadsheet.
